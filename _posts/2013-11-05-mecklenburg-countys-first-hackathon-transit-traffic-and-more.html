Data helps governments pass laws, offer better services, and make informed decisions.Mecklenburg County, NC, is committed to providing residents with clear, accurate information regarding the expenditure of tax dollars for programs and services.Earlier this year, Mecklenburg County, with the City of Charlotte and a few other organizations, introduced the Quality of Life Dashboard.This dashboard helps assess the health of neighborhoods in Charlotte and Mecklenburg County.This citizen-friendly tool is one example of Mecklenburg’s dedication to open data.
