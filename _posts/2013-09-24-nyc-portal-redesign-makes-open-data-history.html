You could hear the collective “ooooh” from the open data community when New York City released its redesigned open data portal yesterday.The Big Apple raised the bar for open government efforts around the world.Besides sharing 200 new datasets, what are some of the new, first-ever features NYC brought to life?Responsive Design Keeping up with how people consume information in 2013, NYC has made their portal easy to browse on a mobile device.Stories and Inspiration Come First Rather than greeting site visitors with a list of available datasets, New York City offers them stories.
