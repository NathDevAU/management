09 Mar 2015 A short, concise, portable description of what your API does, is one of the most critical building blocks of API management, and is essential to reducing friction when new users are on-boarding with any API.It can be easy to design, and populate your API developer portal from a perspective that is very much in the know.I do it all the time, structuring your portal to reach the widest possible audience can be hard—it is why I’m here!;-) Here is a great example of providing all the right elements, but leaving out the essential detail that new API consumers will need.View the Dailymotion developer area, land on home page, and without clicking, tell me what Dailymotion does.
