Something profound is happening to the world economy.Our traditional understanding of the Information Economy is giving way to a new variation that will have an enormous impact on jobs, wealth, and entire industries.This new economy has its roots in data, but of equal importance are two other components: Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) and Mobile Apps.There is also a fourth factor working as an accelerator on each of the three others: the Internet of Things.The business entry point for this new economy is low.
