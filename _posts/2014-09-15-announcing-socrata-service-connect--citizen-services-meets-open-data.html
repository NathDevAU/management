In many U.S.and Canadian communities, the telephone number 311 is reserved for access to non-emergency municipal services and is designed to divert routine inquiries and urgent community concerns from 911 emergency services.The systems that power a 311 call center have become a treasure trove of open data possibilities.  Welcome the Socrata Service Connect™ application!
