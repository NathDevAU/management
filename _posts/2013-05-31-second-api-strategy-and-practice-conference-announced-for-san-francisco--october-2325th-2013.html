After a sold out first event in New York in February 2013, the second edition of the API Strategy and Practice Conference is being announced today and will take place in San Francisco, California from October 23 – 25, 2013.The event will take place at the Parc55 Hotel, located near Union Square, and attendee registration will begin on June 5.Spread over two and a half days, the second edition will build on the success of the New York event with speakers including: Daniel Jacobson, Director of API Engineering at Netflix Themed sessions in the program include sector areas such as: Music, Social, Media, Government, Payments and E-Commerce APIs, as well as deep dives into API Security, Scalability, Business Models, Marketing and Methodology.Tickets, including early-bird discounted tickets, will be available in limited batches starting on the June 5.This event is made possible by the generous support of sponsors including 540.
