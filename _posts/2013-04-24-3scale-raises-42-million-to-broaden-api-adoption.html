SAN FRANCISO, CA – April 24, 2013 – 3scale (http://www.3scale.net), the San Francisco and Barcelona-based API Management solutions provider, today announced $4.2 million of new funding in a round from Javelin Venture Partners and Costanoa Venture Capital.APIs are a key infrastructure component for online businesses – powering mobile applications, partnership programs and innovation.
