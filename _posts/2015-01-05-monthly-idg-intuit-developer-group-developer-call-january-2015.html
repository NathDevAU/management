IPP Developer Lon Orenstein, from Dashboards+Sync for QuickBooks and Microsoft CRM and Map Business Online hosts a monthly developer call for IPP developers.Per Lon: “The purpose is for developers to get together and talk about things specific to our issues (especially technical ones), how we run our business, how we deal with Intuit, and any other topics you’d like to bring up that are common to software companies who have Apps that work with Intuit’s products.” Date: Tuesday, January 6, 2015 Start Time: 3:00 PM Central Std Time End Time: 4:00 PM Central Std Time  2.  Use your microphone and speakers (VoIP) – a headset is recommended.  Or, call in using your telephone.
