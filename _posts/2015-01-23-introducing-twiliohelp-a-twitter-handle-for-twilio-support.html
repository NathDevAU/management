When you reach out to us for help, we want to help as quickly as possible.We’re here and we’re listening no matter what communication channel you use: fax, carrier pigeon, telegram, or Twilio Bat-Signal.Today, we’d like to introduce to a new handle on a very familiar channel.@TwilioHelp is the new Twitter handle that connects you to Twilio Support directly.Whether your issue can be handled in 140 characters or less, or you need more in depth support, @TwilioHelp is the place to go.
