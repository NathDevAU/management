What is happening?On June 1st, 2016 Intuit will discontinue all support for the QuickBooks Online v2 REST API. After June 1st, 2016 applications will no longer be allowed to make v2 API Calls.Why is this happening?About a year ago, Intuit announced the availability of v3  of the QuickBooks Online REST API.
