If America loves a comeback, Chris Wilson’s life is the great American story.After serving 14 years of a life sentence, Wilson is now a business owner (he owns town businesses, actually), a full time community workforce developer, an Entrepreneurs Fellow at the University of Baltimore, and (yes, even) a fitness model.Recently, he was bemoaning the sorry state of raising capital for his own businesses, as well as for the people he helps move off the street and into jobs.“We have revenue coming in,” Wilson told me, “But we need working capital.Small amounts of money, maybe $3,000 to $5,000 and banks all say ‘no’ to that.
