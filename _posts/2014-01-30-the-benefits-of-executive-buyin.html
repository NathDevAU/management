The most successful open data programs in the world share a common advantage: executive buy-in.The full commitment and support of a leader brings three specific advantages to any open data effort: vision, resources, and advocacy.Chicago Mayor Rahm Emanuel, Maryland Governor Martin O’Mallley, and former New York City Mayor Michael Bloomberg have exemplified strong leadership in all of these areas.We’ll highlight a few of them here.Vision An open data program not only serves every citizen, it requires participation from nearly every department in a government organization.
