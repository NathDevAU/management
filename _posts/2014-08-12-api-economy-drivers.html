Gartner predicts that by the end of 2014 75% of the Fortune 1000 will offer public Web APIs and that by 2016 50% of B2B collaboration will take place through Web APIs.Whether or not these predictions come to pass to make such predictions shows the incredible growth rate of Web APIs (also called Business APIs), as we create an API Economy.What is driving this rapid adoption of APIs?The answer is a need for Speed and Reach.Businesses need to respond quickly to market demands and opportunities with little impact to their existing systems.
