Apple, Inc.and IBM Corporation have teamed up to provide mobile solutions for enterprise users.They have joined forces with this partnership to bring a new class of enterprise mobility solutions – with devices, services, security, analytics, and integration – to exploit the full potential of mobility.The partnership spans delivering new apps for the enterprise, device supply & management, service and also support.While collaboration between companies to achieve a common business objective is very common, this particular partnership is specially interesting because it involves APIs along with mobile, big data, cloud and analytics.
