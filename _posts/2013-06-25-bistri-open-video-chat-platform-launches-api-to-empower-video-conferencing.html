San Francisco, CA – (June 25, 2013) – Bistri, the fast growing cloud-based communication platform based on WebRTC, has chosen 3scale (www.3scale.net), the creator of Out-of-the-Box API management infrastructure, to launch, manage, and productize Bistri’s API.Bistri offers the most complete suite of communication tools using the new WebRTC technology inside the web browser.Bistri’s service includes screen instant messaging, presence, notifications, voice and video chat, file transfer, and private storage space.
